## Installation

## How to use?
You can customize and create your own original portfolio by taking this portfolio as reference. Take on various challenges such as changing colors, adding effects, and changing designs. Just include ABOUT, PROJECTS, and CONTACT information in your portfolio and don't get too complicated. Simple is the best.
